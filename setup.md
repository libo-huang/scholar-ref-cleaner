**Scholar Ref Cleaner** is a powerful Streamlit application designed to detect and fix "hallucinated" citations generated by AI tools (like ChatGPT, Claude, DeepSeek). It validates references against global academic databases using a **Waterfall Strategy**.

* **ğŸŒŠ Waterfall Search Strategy**: Prioritizes **Google Scholar** (Most comprehensive) â†’ Auto-switch to **Semantic Scholar** (Stable/Free) â†’ Fallback to **Crossref** (Official Publisher Data).
* **ğŸ¤– Anti-Hallucination**: Automatically flags citations with low similarity matches (< 50%) as potential AI hallucinations.
* **ğŸ“ Multi-Format Support**:
  * **BibTeX (.bib)**: Auto-corrects metadata (Year, Author, Venue) and generates a clean download.
  * **Word/Text (.docx, .txt)**: Scans text lines for citations and generates a validation report.
* **ğŸ“Š Real-time Dashboard**: Visual progress bar, Verified counter, and **ETA (Estimated Time Remaining)**.
* **ğŸŒ Bilingual UI**: One-click switch between English and Chinese.
